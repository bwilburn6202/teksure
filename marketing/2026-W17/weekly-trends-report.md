## Top 5 Trending Tech Topics for Adults 50+

### 1. Post-Tax-Day Refund Scams (HOT)
Tax Day was April 15. The two weeks after are peak season for fake "IRS refund" texts and "tax return error" robocalls. AARP and FTC are both pushing alerts this week. Seniors are getting hit hardest.
**Angle for TekSure:** "If you got a text about your tax refund this week, read this first."

### 2. AI Voice Cloning Scams ("Grandparent 2.0")
FTC issued a fresh consumer alert this month about scammers using 3-second voice clips from social media to fake a grandchild's voice in distress calls. Big news cycle, low public understanding.
**Angle:** Explain in plain English how it works + the family safe word fix.

### 3. Spring "Phone Cleanup" Searches
Google Trends shows spikes in "how to free up storage on iPhone" and "delete old photos" every April. Seniors search this when their phone says "Storage Almost Full."
**Angle:** Quick Win pillar — 5-minute cleanup guide.

### 4. Medicare Open Enrollment Pre-Shopping
Open enrollment is October, but April–May is when seniors start researching plans online. Scammers know this and run fake "Medicare benefits" ad campaigns.
**Angle:** How to spot a real Medicare site vs. a scam ad.

### 5. Mother's Day Tech Gifts (May 10)
Adult kids start searching "best tech gift for mom" 2–3 weeks out. TekSure can capture the *caregiver* audience here — the 40–55 demo who buys for parents.
**Angle:** Caregiver-targeted gift guide that drives newsletter signups.
