# Hook Generator — TekSure

## Purpose
Creates compelling guide titles and social media opening lines that grab attention without resorting to clickbait or fear tactics.

## When to Use
- Naming new guides
- Creating social media posts from guides
- Writing newsletter subject lines
- Crafting homepage featured content

## Hook Formulas
1. **Problem-Solution:** "[Problem]? Here's how to fix it."
2. **How-To:** "How to [accomplish goal] in [number] steps"
3. **Did-You-Know:** "Did you know [surprising fact]? Here's what to do about it."
4. **Empowerment:** "You CAN [task] — here's exactly how"
5. **Safety:** "Protect yourself from [threat] with this one change"

## Examples
- "Locked out of your phone? Here's exactly what to do."
- "How to spot a scam text in 5 seconds"
- "Your phone is tracking you — here's how to stop it"
- "Never fall for a fake email again"
- "The one setting every iPhone owner should turn on today"

## TekSure-Specific Rules
- No fear-mongering — empower, don't frighten
- No clickbait — deliver on the promise
- Plain English — no jargon in hooks
- American context

## Constraints
- Social hooks: under 100 characters
- Guide titles: under 70 characters
- No ALL CAPS, no excessive punctuation
- No "You won't believe..." or "Shocking..." formulas
