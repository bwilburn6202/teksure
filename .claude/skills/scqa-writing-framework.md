# SCQA Writing Framework — TekSure

## Purpose
Transforms tech help topics into structured, engaging guides that hook the reader with a relatable problem and deliver a clear solution.

## When to Use
- Writing new guides for src/data/guides.ts
- Structuring blog posts or educational content
- Creating guide descriptions and excerpts

## Framework
### Situation — Set the scene
"You're trying to [task]" or "You just got a [device]"
- Establish what the reader wants to accomplish
- Keep it relatable and specific

### Complication — Name the problem
"But [common frustration]" or "Then you see [confusing error]"
- Describe exactly what's going wrong
- Use the reader's own words — how would they describe it?

### Question — Frame the ask
"How do you [solve it]?" or "What do you do when [problem]?"
- This is what the reader is searching for
- Match the exact phrasing someone would type into Google

### Answer — Deliver the steps
Step-by-step solution with tips and warnings
- Each step is one clear action
- Include official source links and video references
- End with what success looks like

## TekSure Example
- **S:** "You want to send a photo to your grandchild"
- **C:** "But when you tap the photo, you can't figure out how to share it"
- **Q:** "How do you share a photo from your phone?"
- **A:** [Step 1: Open your Photos app...] [Step 2: Tap the photo...] [Step 3: Tap the Share button...]

## Constraints
- No skipped sections — all four parts required
- No repetition between sections
- Complication must be a REAL problem people actually have
