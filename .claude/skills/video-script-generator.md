# Video Script Generator — TekSure

## Purpose
Converts TekSure guides into short video scripts suitable for YouTube, social media, or embedded tutorials.

## When to Use
- Creating video content from existing guides
- Planning tutorial video series
- Generating scripts for screen recordings

## Script Structure (60-90 seconds)

### Hook (5 seconds)
State the problem directly:
- "Ever gotten locked out of your email? Here's exactly what to do."
- "Think you'd never fall for a scam? Watch this."

### Walkthrough (50-70 seconds)
Screen-by-screen steps:
- "First, open [app]. You'll see [screen description]."
- "Now tap [button]. It's the [color/position] one."
- "Next, [action]. You should see [confirmation]."
- Include pauses for viewer to follow along

### Recap (10 seconds)
What you accomplished + CTA:
- "And that's it — you've [accomplished task]."
- "For more step-by-step guides, visit TekSure.com."

## TekSure-Specific Rules
- Speak like you're helping a friend — warm, patient, clear
- Describe what things look like on screen ("the blue button that says...")
- Never rush — pause between steps
- American English, American services
- Always include accessibility notes ("If you can't see the text, try...")

## Constraints
- Max 90 seconds per script
- One topic per video
- No background music references (keep scripts standalone)
