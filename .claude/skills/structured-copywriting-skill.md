# Structured Copywriting — TekSure

## Purpose
Produces persuasive, accessible copy for TekSure pages that feels helpful rather than salesy.

## When to Use
- Writing landing page sections
- Creating feature descriptions
- Crafting CTAs and promotional content
- Writing page headers and hero sections

## Instructions
1. Open with a hook that names the reader's problem
2. Organize benefits clearly (not features — benefits)
3. Include a warm, specific CTA
4. Close with reassurance

## TekSure-Specific Rules
- Tone: helpful neighbor, not car salesman
- CTAs: "Get started for free" / "Read the guide" / "Try it now" — not "Buy now" or "Sign up"
- Always mention it's free where applicable
- Focus on empowerment: "You CAN do this"
- American audience, American services

## Constraints
- No pressure tactics or artificial urgency
- No jargon
- Readability first — short sentences, clear structure
