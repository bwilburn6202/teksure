# TekSure Marketing Autopilot

**Purpose:** Generate one full week of marketing content for TekSure.com (a free tech help platform for adults 50+ with 236+ guides, 12K members, and 2K newsletter subscribers).

**Trigger:** Run every Monday morning to produce that week's content calendar.

---

## TekSure Brand Guidelines

### Visual Identity
- **Primary Color:** Teal (#33998B)
- **Secondary Color:** Navy (#0D1526)
- **Font:** DM Sans
- **Tone:** Friendly, warm, no-jargon, empathetic

### Voice Rules (Non-Negotiable)
- **Reading Level:** 6th-8th grade (clear, short sentences)
- **Language:** Plain English only—zero jargon
- **Approach:** Empathetic first, then solution-focused
- **Avoid:** "utilize," "leverage," "seamless," "cutting-edge," "robust," "synergy"
- **Preferred:** "step by step," "plain English," "real person," "no jargon," "free," "easy"
- **Tone:** Never pushy or salesy—always helpful and reassuring

### Target Audience
- Adults 50+, seniors, non-technical beginners
- Value: accessibility, free help, no judgment, clear instructions
- Pain points: tech anxiety, complexity, jargon overload

---

## Content Pillars (5 Core Themes)

1. **Quick Wins** — Fast fixes anyone can do in 5 minutes
2. **Education** — Demystify AI, passwords, WiFi, devices, security
3. **Community** — Member questions, polls, success stories, testimonials
4. **Trust** — Social proof, stats, safety tips, brand reassurance
5. **Newsletter Promo** — Call-to-action to grow email subscriber list

## Top SEO Keywords (Priority Targets)

- "what is AI" (110K/mo)
- "how to use ChatGPT" (27K/mo)
- "how to fix WiFi" (4.4K/mo)
- Plus secondary targets in each week's brief

## Weekly Content Deliverables

### 1. Social Media Posts (`weekly-social-posts.md`)
**Facebook (7 posts, 1/day):**
- Mix of Quick Wins, Education, Community, and Trust pillars
- 150–250 words, conversational
- Include CTA where appropriate ("Drop a comment if...", "Share your tip...")
- Link to relevant TekSure guides when applicable

**Instagram (5 posts, 5x/week):**
- Captions 80–150 words, punchy
- Include 3–5 relevant hashtags (#TekSureHelp #TechForSeniors #AIExplained etc.)
- Visual concepts: carousel, static quote, step-by-step graphic, poll slide, member spotlight
- CTA: "Save this for later," "Tag someone who needs this," "DM us your question"

**TikTok (3 post concepts, 3x/week):**
- Short-form concept descriptions (30–60 seconds)
- Hook-script-payoff structure
- Topics: quick tips, myth-busting, relatable frustrations, before/after moments
- CTA: "Follow for more free tech tips"

### 2. SEO Blog Posts (`weekly-blog-draft.md`)
**Target:** 1–2 posts, 800–1,200 words each, SEO-optimized

Structure for each post:
1. **Title:** Include target keyword naturally
2. **Meta Description:** 155–160 chars with keyword
3. **Opening:** Hook with relatable problem (empathy-first)
4. **Body:** Step-by-step instructions, plain English, short paragraphs
5. **Visual Notes:** Suggested screenshots, diagrams, callout boxes
6. **CTA:** Link to TekSure guide or newsletter signup
7. **Internal Links:** Cross-reference 2–3 other TekSure guides

**Blog Topic Selection Rules:**
- Align with weekly trending tech topics for seniors
- Target high-volume keywords from current SEO brief
- Address pain points from community questions
- Avoid overly technical topics—keep 6th-8th grade level
### 3. Email Newsletter (`weekly-email-newsletter.html`)
**Send:** Every Sunday evening to 2K+ subscribers

**Format:**
- **Subject Line:** "This Week's Tech Tip: [Topic]" (keep under 50 chars)
- **Preview Text:** 40–60 chars, teaser of main tip
- **Main Content:** 1 featured Tech Tip summarized to 150–200 words with step-by-step instructions
- **Secondary:** Featured guide callout or community highlight
- **CTA Button:** "Learn More" → relevant guide or blog post
- **Design:** Teal + navy, centered, mobile-responsive, readable font

### 4. SEO Keyword Brief (`weekly-seo-brief.md`)
- 5–8 high-opportunity keywords with search volume, difficulty, TekSure fit
- Trending topics audit (what seniors are searching for this week)
- Opportunities missed by competitors
- Recommendations for next week's blog/social topics

### 5. Trends & Competitor Scan (`weekly-trends-report.md`)
- Top 3–5 trending tech topics for seniors
- Competitor content gaps
- Community pain points from TekSure members or similar platforms
- Seasonal/timely angles (tax scam season, new device releases, privacy alerts)
- Recommendations for next 4 weeks

## Execution Checklist

### Step 1: Research & Trends (30 min)
- Web search: trending tech topics for adults 50+ this week
- Check Reddit, Facebook groups, senior tech forums
- Audit competitor content (YouTube channels, senior tech blogs)
- Identify 1–2 SEO opportunities from high-volume keyword list

### Step 2: SEO Blog Draft (45 min)
- Choose 1–2 topics from research + keyword brief
- Write full blog post(s) with meta description, headers, internal links
- Ensure 6th-8th grade reading level
- Save to `weekly-blog-draft.md`

### Step 3: Social Media Content (45 min)
- Draft 7 Facebook posts, 5 Instagram captions + visual concepts, 3 TikTok scripts
- Mix all 5 content pillars across posts
- Add CTAs and links where relevant
- Save to `weekly-social-posts.md`
### Step 4: Email Newsletter (30 min)
- Choose 1 Tech Tip from this week's blog/research
- Write 150–200 word summary with step-by-step instructions
- Create HTML template (teal/navy, mobile-responsive)
- Save to `weekly-email-newsletter.html`

### Step 5: SEO Keyword Brief (20 min)
- Extract 5–8 high-opportunity keywords from research
- Add search volume, difficulty, TekSure fit for each
- Save to `weekly-seo-brief.md`

### Step 6: Trends Report (20 min)
- Summarize top 3–5 trending topics for seniors
- Note competitor content and gaps
- Recommend next 4 weeks' priorities
- Save to `weekly-trends-report.md`

## Quality Checklist Before Saving

- All content uses plain English, 6th-8th grade level
- No jargon or "corporate" language
- Empathetic tone, solution-focused approach
- CTAs are clear and non-pushy
- Brand colors (teal #33998B, navy #0D1526) referenced in email/visual notes
- All content aligned with 5 content pillars
- SEO keywords naturally woven into blog/social
- Internal cross-links added to blog posts
- Mobile-responsive design for email
- Links to TekSure guides are accurate and relevant

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Start with empathy.** Every post should begin with a relatable problem.
2. **Keep it scannable.** Short paragraphs, bullet points, bold key terms.
3. **Test for readability.** Read aloud; if it sounds stuffy, simplify.
4. **Mix pillars.** Spread Quick Wins, Trust, Community across the week.
5. **Link internally.** Always direct to TekSure guides or newsletter signup.
6. **Community first.** Highlight member stories—it builds trust and engagement.
7. **Track performance.** Note which topics get high engagement for next week.
