# Long-Form Summary Compressor — TekSure

## Purpose
Reduces full guide content into concise excerpts for search results, cards, and meta tags.

## When to Use
- Creating or fixing guide excerpts (must be under 160 characters)
- Generating meta descriptions for SEO
- Writing card summaries for guide listings

## Instructions
1. Read the full guide content (steps, tips, warnings)
2. Identify the ONE key outcome the reader achieves
3. Write the excerpt as: "Learn how to [outcome] with [method] — step by step."
4. Verify character count is under 160
5. Ensure it makes sense standalone (no context needed)

## Output Format
- **Excerpt:** Under 160 chars, describes the guide's value
- **Meta description:** Under 155 chars, SEO-optimized
- **Card summary:** Under 80 chars, for compact card views

## TekSure-Specific Rules
- Plain English — no jargon in summaries
- Lead with the benefit, not the topic
- Good: "Stop scam calls and texts from reaching your phone in 5 easy steps."
- Bad: "This guide covers call blocking functionality on mobile devices."

## Constraints
- Never exceed 160 characters for excerpts
- No filler words
- Must be self-contained (understandable without reading the guide)
