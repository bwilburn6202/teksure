# Competitive Intelligence — TekSure

## Purpose
Identifies what competitor sites cover that TekSure doesn't, and where TekSure can differentiate.

## When to Use
- Monthly competitive intelligence reports
- Planning new guide topics or features
- Evaluating TekSure's market position

## Competitors to Track
| Site | Strength | Gap |
|------|----------|-----|
| AARP Tech | Brand trust, large audience | Less hands-on guides |
| GCFGlobal | Free courses, structured learning | Less current, no troubleshooting |
| Senior Planet | Live classes, hotline | Limited self-serve content |
| TechBoomers | Beginner-focused | Dated design, fewer guides |
| How-To Geek | Deep technical content | Not senior-focused |

## Analysis Framework
1. **Content coverage:** What topics do they have that we don't?
2. **Content depth:** Where is their content deeper/shallower?
3. **Format:** Do they offer video, interactive tools, printable guides?
4. **Freshness:** How current is their content?
5. **Accessibility:** How senior-friendly is their design?

## Output Format
- Content gaps (topics we're missing)
- Opportunities (where we can do better)
- Recommended new guides (top 10 by priority)
- Recommended new features/tools

## Constraints
- Focus on actionable insights, not generic comparisons
- Compare content quality, not just quantity
