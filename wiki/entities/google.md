# Google Services

Google offers a large set of free tools that all connect through **one Google account**. When you sign up for Gmail, you automatically get access to Drive, Calendar, Maps, and more. Your Google account is your email address (usually ending in @gmail.com) and one password.

## Core Services

- **Gmail** — Free email. Access it at gmail.com or through the Gmail app.
- **Google Drive** — Free cloud storage (15 GB). Save documents, photos, and files online so you can reach them from any device.
- **Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides** — Free alternatives to Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. You can create and edit documents right in your web browser.
- **Google Calendar** — Keep track of appointments and events. You can set reminders that notify your phone.
- **Google Maps** — Get directions, find nearby stores, and explore street views.
- **Google Meet** — Free video calls. Start a meeting from Gmail or meet.google.com.
- **Google Chrome** — A web browser made by Google. It syncs your bookmarks and passwords across devices when you sign in.
- **Google Photos** — Store and organize your photos and videos with free storage (counts toward your 15 GB).

## How They Connect

Sign in once and everything shares the same account. An event added to Calendar can send a Google Meet link by email through Gmail. A file saved in Drive can be attached in Gmail. This is the advantage of one connected account.
