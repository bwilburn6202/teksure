# Apple Ecosystem

Apple makes the iPhone, iPad, and Mac computers. All Apple devices work together through your **Apple Account** (formerly called Apple ID), which connects your email, photos, purchases, and settings across every device you own.

## Key Devices

- **iPhone** — Apple's smartphone. Used for calls, texts, photos, apps, and internet browsing.
- **iPad** — A tablet with a larger screen. Great for reading, video calls, and watching videos.
- **Mac** — Apple's desktop and laptop computers, running macOS.

## Important Features

- **iCloud** — Apple's cloud storage. It backs up your photos, contacts, and files automatically. You get 5 GB free.
- **FaceTime** — Free video and audio calls to other Apple users. Works over Wi-Fi or cellular data.
- **App Store** — Where you download apps to your iPhone or iPad. Look for the blue icon with a white "A."
- **iMessage** — Apple's texting service. Messages between Apple devices show up in blue bubbles.
- **Siri** — Apple's voice assistant. Say "Hey Siri" to ask questions, set reminders, or make calls.

## Common Tasks

- **Update your device:** Go to Settings > General > Software Update.
- **Take a screenshot:** Press the Side button and Volume Up at the same time.
- **Find a lost device:** Use the [[Find My]] app or visit icloud.com/find.
