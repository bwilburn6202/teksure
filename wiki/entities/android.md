# Android Phones and Tablets

Android is the operating system (the software that runs your phone) made by Google. It is used by many manufacturers, including **Samsung**, **Google Pixel**, **Motorola**, and **OnePlus**. Your Android device is tied to your **Google account**, which connects your email, apps, photos, and settings.

## Popular Android Brands

- **Samsung Galaxy** — The most popular Android phones and tablets worldwide.
- **Google Pixel** — Made by Google with fast software updates and a great camera.
- **Motorola** — Known for affordable, reliable phones.

## Key Features

- **Google Play Store** — Where you download apps. Look for the colorful triangle icon.
- **Google Assistant** — Say "Hey Google" to ask questions, set alarms, or send texts.
- **Google Photos** — Backs up your photos and videos to the cloud automatically.
- **Settings** — Swipe down from the top of the screen, then tap the gear icon to open Settings.

## Common Tasks

- **Update your phone:** Settings > System > Software Update (may vary by brand).
- **Take a screenshot:** Press the Power button and Volume Down at the same time.
- **Uninstall an app:** Long-press the app icon, then tap "Uninstall."
- **Connect to Wi-Fi:** Settings > Network & Internet > Wi-Fi, then pick your network.
