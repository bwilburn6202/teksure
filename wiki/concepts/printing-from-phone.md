# Printing from Your Phone

You can print photos, emails, web pages, and documents straight from your phone or tablet. Here is how.

## What You Need

- A **Wi-Fi printer** — most modern printers connect to your home Wi-Fi network.
- Your phone and printer must be connected to the **same Wi-Fi network**. This is the most important step.

## Printing from an iPhone or iPad (AirPrint)

Most newer printers support **AirPrint**, which lets Apple devices print without installing anything extra.

1. Open the document, photo, or web page you want to print.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the box with an arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Print**.
4. Tap **Select Printer** and choose your printer from the list.
5. Pick how many copies you want.
6. Tap **Print** in the top-right corner.

## Printing from an Android Phone

1. Open what you want to print.
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner.
3. Tap **Print** or **Share** > **Print**.
4. Select your printer from the dropdown at the top.
5. Tap the **Print** button.

If your printer does not show up, you may need to download your printer's app from the Google Play Store (for example, **HP Smart** or **Epson iPrint**).

## Troubleshooting

- **Printer not showing up?** Make sure both your phone and printer are on the same [[connecting-to-wifi|Wi-Fi network]].
- **Still not working?** Turn your printer off and back on. Then try again.
- **Paper jam?** Open the printer, gently remove the stuck paper, close it, and try again.

**Quick Tip:** If you are at a library or office supply store (like Staples or FedEx), you can often email a document to their printer or use their app to print from your phone.
