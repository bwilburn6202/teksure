# Freeing Up Storage

When your phone or computer says "Storage Full," it means there is no more room for new photos, apps, or updates. Here is how to make space.

## Check How Much Storage You Have

**iPhone:**
1. Open **Settings** > **General** > **iPhone Storage**.
2. You will see a colored bar showing what is using space.

**Android:**
1. Open **Settings** > **Storage**.
2. You will see a breakdown of what is taking up room.

## Delete Old Photos and Videos

Videos and photos take up the most space. Go through your camera roll and delete ones you do not need. Videos use far more space than photos.

**Quick Tip:** Back up your photos to [[what-is-the-cloud|the cloud]] first (iCloud, Google Photos, or OneDrive). That way you still have copies even after deleting them from your phone.

## Remove Apps You Do Not Use

Look through your apps. If you have not opened an app in months, delete it.

- **iPhone:** Press and hold the app icon, then tap **Remove App** > **Delete App**.
- **Android:** Press and hold the app icon, then tap **Uninstall**.

You can always download the app again later from the App Store or Google Play Store if you need it.

## Clear Browser Data

Your web browser saves old data that piles up over time.

- Open your browser (Safari, Chrome, etc.).
- Go to **Settings** within the browser.
- Look for **Clear Browsing Data** or **Clear History and Website Data**.

## Use Cloud Storage

Move files, photos, and documents to a cloud service like iCloud, Google Drive, or OneDrive. This keeps them safe and frees up space on your device. See [[what-is-the-cloud]] for more details.
