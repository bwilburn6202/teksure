# Streaming and Entertainment

Streaming means watching shows, movies, or listening to music over the internet — without buying a disc or downloading a file. The content plays in real time, like turning on a TV channel.

## What You Need

1. **An internet connection** — streaming video uses a lot of data, so a reliable Wi-Fi connection is important.
2. **A device** — a smart TV, streaming stick (Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV, Chromecast), phone, tablet, or computer.
3. **A streaming app or service** — some are free, some require a monthly subscription.

## Free vs Paid Services

**Free streaming services** (no subscription needed):
- **Tubi** — thousands of movies and shows, supported by short ads
- **Pluto TV** — live TV channels and on-demand content, free with ads
- **YouTube** — videos on every topic, free (some content is ad-supported)
- **PBS** — free documentaries and shows from public television

**Paid streaming services** (monthly fee):
- **Netflix** ($7-23/month) — large library of shows, movies, and documentaries
- **Hulu** ($8-18/month) — current TV shows plus movies
- **Disney+** ($8-14/month) — Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic
- **Amazon Prime Video** (included with $15/month Prime membership)
- **Max** ($10-17/month) — HBO shows, movies, and documentaries

## Streaming Devices

If your TV is not a smart TV, you can add streaming with a small plug-in device:
- **Roku** — affordable, works with all services, remote with dedicated buttons
- **Amazon Fire TV Stick** — works with Alexa voice commands
- **Apple TV** — best if you use other Apple devices
- **Chromecast** — send video from your phone to the TV

All of these plug into your TV's HDMI port and connect to your Wi-Fi.

## Common Issues

- **Buffering (spinning circle)** — your internet connection may be slow. Move closer to your router or restart it.
- **Cannot find an app** — look in your device's app store. Most streaming apps are free to download.
- **Forgot your password** — use the "Forgot Password" option on the service's login page.
