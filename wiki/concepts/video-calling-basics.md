# Video Calling Basics

A video call lets you see and talk to someone on your phone, tablet, or computer — like a phone call with a live picture. Here is how to get started.

## FaceTime (iPhone and iPad)

1. Open the **FaceTime** app (a green icon with a white video camera).
2. Tap the **+** button or type a person's name or phone number in the search bar.
3. Tap the **Video** button (it looks like a camera icon).
4. Wait for them to answer. You will see their face on your screen and yours in a small box in the corner.

FaceTime works between Apple devices — iPhones, iPads, and Macs.

## Zoom

If someone sends you a Zoom link (by text or email):
1. Tap the link.
2. The Zoom app will open (download it free from the App Store or Google Play Store if you do not have it).
3. Tap **Join with Video** and **Join with Audio** when asked.

To start your own Zoom call, open the app and tap **New Meeting**.

## Google Meet

1. Open the **Google Meet** app or go to meet.google.com in your browser.
2. Tap **New Meeting** to start a call, or enter a meeting code someone gave you.
3. Tap **Join Now**.

## During the Call

- **Mute your microphone:** Tap the microphone icon to turn your sound on or off. This is helpful if there is background noise.
- **Turn your camera on or off:** Tap the camera icon.
- **Switch cameras:** Tap the rotate/flip camera icon to switch between the front and back camera.
- **End the call:** Tap the red phone or **Leave** button.

**Quick Tip:** Prop your phone or tablet against something steady so you do not have to hold it the whole time.
