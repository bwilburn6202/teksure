🧓 What does this actually do? (Plain-English)

When you paste a link, TekSure asks four different safety services at once: Google's Safe Browsing list (which tracks known bad pages), PhishTank (a community list of scam sites), URLVoid (a third-party reputation check), and Sucuri (which scans for hacked pages). We combine the answers and tell you one simple thing: green, yellow, or red. We do not save the link you paste. We do not click through. You decide what to do with the answer.
